Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the ML matching? +

Our proprietary matching algorithm uses high-dimensional vector embeddings to analyze your skills. It currently maintains a 94% correlation with real-world hiring manager feedback across 50,000+ benchmarked profiles.

Can I upload a PDF resume? +

Yes! Pro users can upload PDF, DOCX, and TXT files. Our AI automatically parses your experience, projects, and skills to build your digital skill vector instantly.

Does SkillMap integrate with LinkedIn? +

Absolutely. You can import your LinkedIn profile with one click to seed your initial skill map. We also provide a "Profile Optimizer" that suggests specific keywords to add to your LinkedIn for maximum visibility.

Is there a student discount available? +

We offer a 50% lifetime discount for students with a valid .edu email address. We believe career intelligence should be accessible to everyone starting their journey.

How often is the career database updated? +

Our data team updates role requirements weekly by scraping thousands of new job postings from Top-tier tech companies to ensure your roadmap reflects current market demands.

Can I export my roadmap to Notion? +

Yes, SkillMap supports direct export to Notion, Trello, and PDF. You can take your personalized roadmap wherever you track your learning progress.

Is my data secure and private? +

Your privacy is our priority. We use AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and do not share your personal information or resume content with third-party recruiters without your explicit consent.
